# Google Invented AI's Foundation, Then Froze

These sources collectively trace a painful arc: Google built the intellectual and infrastructural foundations of modern AI — the Transformer, the talent, the compute — yet stumbled when that technology became disruptive rather than sustaining. Publishing 'Attention Is All You Need' was simultaneously one of history's greatest gifts to humanity and a catastrophic corporate own-goal. The historical pattern is telling: IBM lost to Microsoft, Microsoft lost to Google on the web, but Google survived mobile. Whether it survives AI is genuinely uncertain. The difference this time is that the threat comes from inside the house — Google must cannibalize its $140B search machine to compete with tools built on its own research.
