## Key Arguments

1. **SaaS re-rating is sentiment, not
   fundamentals.** Top-10 enterprise
   software companies are posting strong
   quarters but trading at 2x revenue.
   The market is pricing in AI disruption
   that hasn't shown up in the numbers yet.

2. **Anthropic won by narrowing, not
   expanding.** While OpenAI chased video,
   ads, and hardware, and Google fumbled
   consumer launches, Anthropic committed
   to coding agents. Claude 4.6 was the
   inflection point where enterprise coding
   actually worked.

3. **Economic entanglement is the peace
   strategy with China.** Bringing CEOs
   to the Xi summit isn't optics — it's
   the mechanism. Bidirectional trade
   dependency reduces conflict incentives
   more reliably than military deterrence.

4. **Multi-sensory, always-on AI is the
   next compute paradigm.** Turn-based
   prompting is being replaced by real-time
   audio/visual/desktop models running
   every 200ms. This will 1000x token
   demand and reshape hardware requirements.

5. **El Niño 2026 is a serious macro risk**
   being ignored outside commodity markets.
   Record ocean energy storage means record
   heat, crop failures in Brazil/India/
   Australia, and potential food-driven
   instability in South and Southeast Asia
   within 12 months.
